AeropuertosGap, is a go-to app for accessing a range of services offered by the 12 airports within the Grupo GAP network:

  • Aguascalientes
  • Guanajuato
  • Guadalajara
  • Hermosillo
  • La Paz
  • Los Mochis
  • Manzanillo
  • Mexicali
  • Morelia
  • Puerto Vallarta
  • San Jos√© del Cabo
  • Tijuana
AeropuertosGap offers
  1. Stay informed about important travel news and receive notifications about authority regulations.
  2. Easily access flight information for all Grupo GAP airports, subscribe to specific flights of interest, and receive updates on their status.
  3. Order food from the app and have it delivered to your flight gate or to designated airport eateries.
  4. Manage your Passport VIP lounge memberships seamlessly through the app.
  5. Conveniently pay for parking tickets directly from your mobile device.
  6. Stay up-to-date with the latest airport news and updates via the Social Feed section of the app.
